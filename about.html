About Us

Home About Us

Spectacles at Catalpa is a top-rated sunglasses and eyeglasses store in Ridgewood, Queens. Serving the Ridgewood community, we’re committed to serving our customers with care. We’re proud to provide you with the most up-to-date eye care at fair prices.

We Speak Your Language

We love serving the Ridgewood community. Our specialists speak English, Russian, Polish and Spanish.

Frames For Every Face, A Style
For Every Occasion

We’ve partnered with Essilor and offer a large collection of lens’ brands for you to choose from! Some of these brands are Varilux, Eyezen, Crizal, Transitions and XPerio. With a wide range of styles, we’re completely confident that we have a pair of sunglasses that are sure to fit the needs and wants of absolutely everyone.

Experience Comfort and Relaxation

We know that choosing a pair of sunglasses and eyeglasses is a process. When you come to Spectacles at Catalpa, you can enjoy browsing and exploring our selection to decide on your next pair of glasses. With comfortable seating and refreshments, as well as friendly staff who can guide you, we make choosing your next pair of eyeglasses or sunglasses a pleasurable experience. We can’t wait for you to come visit us!
